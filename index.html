About Me

Who am I

I'm Mateja Vasojevikj, also known as vasosofuji. I'm a photographer based in Skopje, North Macedonia, that focuses on capturing raw emotions.

I shoot portraits, concerts and various corporate events. Because of my love for music the events I photograph the most are concerts and festivals, but I bring uniqueness to portraits as well.

Collaborations & Gear

  • Artist Collaborations: Vladimir Chetkar, Noviot Pochetok, Marigold Box (Italy), Epidemik (RS), Ana && Stefan Petanovski, Golemata Voda, Fiction and many more
  • Organisations: Zdravo Mladi, Zemjotres, Underfest, Tributefest, Sepak Se Vrti, Stanica26, Marakana, Laboratorium, Enterprise Music School, Kotur, MKC and more
  • Gear: Fujifilm X-T2 and a National PE-145 flash from the 1970's.
