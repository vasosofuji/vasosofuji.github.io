Who am I

I'm Mateja Vasojevikj, also known as vasosofuji, a 19 year old Cybersecurity Student at Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering at "St. Cyril and Methodius" University.

On top of being a student, I'm also a freelance photographer and cinematographer based in Skopje, North Macedonia.
