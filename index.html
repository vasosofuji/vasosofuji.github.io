About Me

Who am I

I'm Mateja Vasojevikj, also known as vasosofuji, a 19 year old Cybersecurity Student at Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering at "Sts. Cyril and Methodius" University.

On top of being a student, I'm also a freelance photographer and cinematographer based in Skopje, North Macedonia.

I shoot Portraits, Landscapes and Events, but my primary focus is Concert Photography (because of my love for music).

If you enjoy any of my work, feel free to contact me or message me on instagram!

Collaborations & Gear

  • Artist Collaborations: Vladimir Chetkar, Noviot Pochetok, Marigold Box (Italy), Epidemik (RS), Ana && Stefan Petanovski, Golemata Voda, Fiction and many more
  • Organisations: Zdravo Mladi, Zemjotres, Underfest, Tributefest, Sepak Se Vrti, Stanica26, Marakana, Laboratorium, Enterprise Music School, Kotur, MKC and more
  • Gear: Fujifilm X-T2 and a National PE-145 flash from the 1970's.
